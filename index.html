Why This Business Works

Four reasons this keeps paying you every month

Internet is no longer a luxury in Uganda. It is a daily need. That demand becomes your income.

Recurring monthly income

Customers pay every day, week, or month for continued access. Your income does not stop when you sleep.

Low operating costs

After setup, your only cost is the internet connection you already pay for. No staff, no stock, no daily hustle.

High internet demand

Students, tenants, traders, and customers all need internet. Demand in Uganda is growing every year.

The hotspot system manages itself. Voucher generation, user limits, and payments can be fully automated.

How It Works

From first call to first income in four steps

Most installations are complete and earning within two to five days of your first enquiry.

Step 1

Free Consultation

Contact us via WhatsApp or phone. Tell us about your property, the number of users you expect, and your income goals. We ask the right questions.

Step 2

Site Survey

Our technician visits your location, checks signal coverage, power availability, and the best router placement to serve all your users effectively.

Step 3

Installation

We supply, mount, and configure all equipment. Routers, access points, cabling, and the hotspot billing system are all installed and tested on the same day.

Step 4

Start Earning

We walk you through the system, show you how to print vouchers, check your dashboard, and handle any issues. Then your WiFi business is live and earning.

Ready to get started? The first step takes less than five minutes.
