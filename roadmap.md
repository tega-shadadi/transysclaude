Design style
I'd recommend something modern and premium:
White background
Very light gray sections
Blue accent color
Large rounded cards (16–24px radius)
Soft shadows
Plenty of whitespace
Smooth animations
SVG icons (not emojis)
Professional typography (Inter or Manrope)
Responsive from mobile to desktop
Think of the visual quality of:
Stripe
Linear
Vercel
Framer
Notion
rather than a typical Bootstrap business website.
